I am a postdoctoral fellow at University of Toronto. I completed my PhD in social psychology at The Ohio State University.

In my work, I address a variety of questions, but two central ones are:

  • What roles do emotions play in how people make decisions?
  • What are the consequences of these choices?

To do this, I use a variety of methods: behavioral experiments, computational modeling, eye-tracking, physiological measures, neuro-imaging, and pharmacological interventions.
